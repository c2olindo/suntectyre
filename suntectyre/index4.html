About Us

Suntec Tyre Limited was established to manufacture all types of automotive three wheeler and two wheeler tyres in our state of the art plant with incomparable quality. In 2012 we started planning the project with Mr. Richard Ong from Pam Hsiang Trading, Taiwan and gradually started its test production on 30th May 2014. We are importing the resources like Rubber and Carbon black from the top most countries like Taiwan, Korea, Japan and China, for delivering best quality product to our customers. We are exporting our tyres in African countries like Morocco and in Sri Lanka for three wheeler. We are having around 1200 shops who are the distributors of our tyres all around country.

All the tyre manufacturing machines are sourced from renowned companies like Yi Tzung, Great Chance, Chiu Ming, etc of Taiwan. Initially, we targeted the wheelbarrow sizes along with motorcycle tyres as these tyres have a huge market in our country. In coming months, we are also plan to produce light truck tyres.
